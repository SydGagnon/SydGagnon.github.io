About Me

Hello! My name is Sydney Gagnon and I'm a junior at Virginia Tech majoring in Multimedia Journalism with a minor in Professional and Technical Writing. I also play goalie for the Hokies' Division 1 lacrosse team. I'm a proud "parront" to my 13-year-old African Grey parrot, Roxie, and the two of us love to read together. You'll discover that I am also very interested in the craft brewing industry, as evidenced by many of my blog posts. Hopefully you'll find something here that interests you as well!
