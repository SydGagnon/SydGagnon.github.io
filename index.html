About Me

Hello! My name is Sydney Gagnon and I'm a junior at Virginia Tech majoring in Multimedia Journalism with a minor in Professional and Technical Writing. I also play goalie for the Hokies' Division 1 lacrosse team. I'm a proud "parront" to my 13-year-old African Grey parrot, Roxie, and the two of us love to read together. You'll discover that I am also very interested in the craft brewing industry, as evidenced by many of my blog posts. Hopefully you'll find something here that interests you as well!


Medium Posts

Here you'll find my various blog posts, all published on Medium. I write about a variety of topics that interest me, ranging from brewing and birds to television and entertainment.

  • "Stop Settling for Basic Brews: an Introductory Guide to the World of Beer"

    Brand new to the world of beer? You've come to the right place! In this article, I provide a crash course on the brewing process as well as teach readers how to describe the beers that they're drinking.

  • "The Unofficial Beer Drinker's Field Guide"

    This article details more differences between the various types of beers. It's excellent for those looking to explore which beers match their specific tastes.

  • "Underrated Shows That Will Restore Your Faith in Reality TV"

    Reality TV definitely comes with its own stigma. If you're more partial to goofy contest shows as opposed to the Kardashians, then these shows are right up your alley.
